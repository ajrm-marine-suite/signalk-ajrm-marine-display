AJRM Marine Display Help
Simulator/test release: AJRM Marine Display is an aid to awareness, not a navigation or collision-avoidance authority. Use the chart, AIS targets, and alerts as supporting information only.

Display is deliberately lean: it draws the chart, shows targets, presents active alerts, and offers the controls needed while sailing. AJRM Marine Traffic, Notifications, Audio, Logger, and Voyage Capture own their deeper settings and diagnostics.

Map controls
  • Centre vessel: recentres on own vessel and resumes following.
  • Chart selector: chooses basemaps, Signal K chart layers, Auto Charts, OpenSeaMap, and harbour limits.
  • AIS Targets: opens the live target list and selected-vessel details.
  • Profiles: selects the active AJRM Marine Traffic profile and adjusts current-profile sensitivity.
  • Settings: controls Display-only options such as alerts, dark mode, no-sleep, own-vessel icon, harbour limits, and TCPA guide lines.
  • Help: opens this quick Display reference.
Alerts shown by Display

Alert severity, CPA/TCPA, profile choice, muting, and speech decisions come from the suite services. Display shows those projections; it does not recalculate traffic safety state.

  • Bottom alert panel: keeps active alerts visible at the bottom of the chart.
  • Popup alerts: shows a modal popup for important alert events.
  • Popup alert sound: plays a short local browser sound for popups when suite audio is not muted.
Charts and following

Auto Charts chooses a chart for the current vessel or map position. Manual panning pauses following; press centre vessel to follow own vessel again. OpenSeaMap is a transparent seamark overlay and is kept above chart tiles.

After an update

If the version changed but the browser still behaves like an older release, hard-refresh the page. On Safari, try Option+Cmd+R on Mac or clear website data for the Signal K hostname on iPhone/iPad.
